# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A complete Great Highland Bagpipe set rendered as a real, finished physical object: a soft tartan-covered reservoir bag at the center, with five turned wooden stocks tied into it and five pipes radiating from it in the classic recognizable silhouette — a short blowpipe with its valve end, a conical melody chanter dropping below the bag with a row of front tone holes and a flared sole, two identical tenor drones and one longer bass drone rising up and back over the player's shoulder line, the drones built in sections joined by hemped tuning slides and capped with sound-exit tops. Turned components in dark, fine-grained African blackwood with a satin oil finish, accented by pale ivory-look Delrin mounts, ferrules, and ring projections at the joints; the bag cover a muted wool tartan. Realistic scale cues: the bass drone reads roughly as long as a forearm, the chanter about the length of a hand-and-a-half. Clean neutral light-grey studio background, soft directional studio light, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

The same Great Highland Bagpipe set staged in a woodturner's workshop on a clean bench: the tartan bag resting with the three drones leaning up over a wooden cradle, the chanter laid in front beside a small maintenance kit of waxed hemp, a roll of natural hemp thread, a block of beeswax, and a couple of spare cane and synthetic reeds. A lathe and turning tools sit softly out of focus in the warm natural window light behind. Shallow depth of field holding the drone tops and chanter sharp, environmental storytelling of an in-progress hand build, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of a single drone tuning-slide joint: two turned blackwood sections meeting at a hemped tenon-and-socket, the wrapped natural hemp thread visible where the upper section telescopes into the lower, a pale Delrin ring projection and ferrule framing the joint, fine wood grain and the slight sheen of beeswax on the hemp. Raking light grazes across the turned beads and the hemp wrap to show texture and the telescoping tuning travel. High detail, photorealistic, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no impossible mechanisms, no extra or missing drones, no electronic parts; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
