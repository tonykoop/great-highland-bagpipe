# Pipe Scale March

**Tradition/source:** Original chanter study
**Public domain:** Yes - pre-1929 US or original MIT
**Arrangement:** Starter L2 arrangement for `great-highland-bagpipe`.

## Range check
Low G-High A: within the beginner starter range for this packet.

## Practice tip
Add doublings only after the plain melody is rhythmically square.
