# Amazing Grace

**Tradition/source:** John Newton / New Britain tune, public domain
**Public domain:** Yes - pre-1929 US or original MIT
**Arrangement:** Starter L2 arrangement for `great-highland-bagpipe`.

## Range check
Low A-High A: within the beginner starter range for this packet.

## Practice tip
Tune drones first, then play slowly enough that every note locks to the drone.

## Known gaps
Audio, MusicXML, LilyPond, and printable songsheet stages are intentionally skipped until the shared render toolchain is installed. Fingering SVGs are static L2 review artifacts and should be checked against the final drilled hole layout before public release.
