# Jig Decision

| Operation | Jig choice | Reason | Release check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chanter turning | Lathe between centers with bore reference | Chanter taper and hole layout need a stable centerline | Bore profile and OD stations are measured before drilling |
| Drone sections | Lathe mandrel and stop collars | Tenor drones must duplicate cleanly | First tenor validates before second is copied |
| Reed-seat work | Hand ream with commercial reed in hand | Reed fit dominates pitch stability | Reed seats without rocking and starts consistently |
| Stock/blowpipe fit | Taper gauges and leak test plugs | Airtight interfaces are mandatory | Bag pressure leak test passes before tuning |
| Hole drilling | Indexed drilling fixture | Chanter holes require angular and station control | Undersize holes match drawing before tuning enlargement |
